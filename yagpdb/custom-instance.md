```regex
  custom(?:om?i(?:ze|se) (?:the )?)?(?:name|p(?:rofile|fp)|banner|icon|avatar|status|instance|bot)|
  private (?:instance|bot)|
  no verif(?:ication|y)|
  bypass verif(?:ication|y)
)
```
